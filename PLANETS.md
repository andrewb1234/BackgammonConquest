# Planetary Node Modifiers

## Purpose
Define node-based passive modifiers affecting campaign and battle states.

---

## Node Definitions

| Node Name           | ID   | Modifier Type | Gameplay Effect |
|--------------------|------|----------------|-----------------|
| Iron Forge VII     | 1    | Economy        | Reduce the purchase cost of all Tactical Items by 20%. |
| The Sightless Eye  | 2    | Information    | Reveal the opponent's pending dice roll values at the start of the player's turn. |
| Helios Gate        | 3    | Initiative     | Automatically win the Initiative Roll for battles initiated from this node. |
| Martyr’s Rest      | 4    | Defense        | Nullify the first successful enemy "hit" against a friendly piece per match. |
| The Maw            | 5    | Loadout        | Increase maximum Tactical Item slots from 2 to 3. |
| The Bastion        | 6/7  | Capital        | Defender may manually change one rolled "6" to a "Double-6" once per match. Defender begins match with 2 pieces already borne off. |
