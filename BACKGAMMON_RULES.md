# Backgammon Rules

## Board
The board has 24 triangular points divided into four quadrants. Each player moves in opposite directions across the board.

## Pieces
Each player has 15 checkers. Movement is determined by rolling two dice.

## Gameplay
Players alternate turns, moving checkers according to dice rolls. Checkers can land on open points, points with their own pieces, or a single opponent checker, which is hit and sent to the bar. Hit checkers must re-enter before other moves.

## Objective
Move all checkers into the home board and bear them off. The first player to remove all checkers wins.

## Doubling Cube
The doubling cube is used to raise stakes. A player may offer a double before rolling; the opponent can accept or resign. Accepted doubles give cube control, allowing future redoubles.
