# Tactical Item Execution and Loadout Systems

## Purpose
Define the usage, timing, and mechanical resolution of consumable items.

---

## Core Rules
- Players may equip a maximum of **2 Tactical Items** in their pre-match loadout  
- Items are purchased using **Void-Scrap** before match start  
- Each item requires a specific **trigger state** for execution  
- Tactical Items are **single-use and session-bound**:
  - Equipping an item commits it to the upcoming match
  - Items cannot be unequipped, refunded, or carried over
  - All equipped items must be either used during the match or are **forfeited on match end**
---

## Item Definitions

| Item Name           | Trigger State              | Execution Logic |
|--------------------|---------------------------|-----------------|
| **Air Strike**      | Player Turn (Pre-Move)    | Select one vulnerable enemy piece (single blot). Move it immediately to the Bar (Void-Buffer). Does not consume movement. |
| **Angelic Protection** | Player Turn (Pre-Move) | Select one friendly piece. Apply invulnerability for 2 turns. Enemy movement targeting this point is blocked and must be recalculated. |
| **Lucky Chance**    | Post-Roll                 | Force a complete re-roll of either the player's or opponent's active dice. Consumed upon use. |
| **Sabotage**        | Pre-Match                 | Select one enemy-controlled planetary node. Disable its passive modifier for the duration of the match. |
